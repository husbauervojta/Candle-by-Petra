My Story

About Petra

From the Mediterranean coast to your home — a story of passion, craft, and scent.

How it began

A passion born in Cyprus

It started as a quiet obsession. I was looking for a candle that truly captured the scent of a Cypriot summer evening — the dry warmth, the jasmine, the salt in the air. When I couldn't find it, I decided to make it myself.

What began as a personal project in my kitchen slowly grew into something much bigger. Friends wanted them. Then strangers. And with each candle I poured, I fell deeper in love with the craft.

Today, every Candles by Petra candle is still made by hand in my studio in Larnaca — in small batches, with the same attention to detail as the very first one I ever made.

What I believe in

The principles behind every candle

Natural Ingredients

I use only natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and premium fragrance oils — no paraffin, no compromise.

Small Batches

Every candle is poured in small batches to ensure consistent quality and the personal touch that mass production can never replicate.

Slow & Intentional

I never rush. Each scent is developed over weeks, tested, and refined until it feels exactly right.

Handcrafted luxury candles made in Larnaca, Cyprus. Small batches, natural ingredients, pure intention.
